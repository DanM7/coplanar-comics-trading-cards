# Coplanar Comics Trading Cards

**Coplanar Comics Trading Cards** is a digital trading card experience set inside the Coplanar Comics Universe — a multi‑plane world spanning Coplanar City, Coplanar Plains, and Parallel Pines. Each character originates from one of four planes: Human, Comic, 2D, or 3D, and each card includes a front (character art, name, tier) and back (bio, stats, lore).

## Features
- Open digital card packs and collect characters from across the Coplanar Comics Universe  
- Flip cards to view front/back designs  
- Track your collection in a digital binder  
- Explore characters from all four planes: Human, Comic, 2D, and 3D  
- Built for future expansion into comic issues, story arcs, and animated shorts

## Universe Overview
The Coplanar Comics Universe spans three major locations:
- **Coplanar City** — a dimensional metropolis with districts for each plane  
- **Coplanar Plains** — a rural region where anomalies leak into everyday life  
- **Parallel Pines** — a forested area where geometry and nature collide  

Each location contains four districts aligned to the four planes:
- Human: Realspace Row, Old Coplanar, Pine Hollow  
- Comic: Panel Park, Gag Gulch, Punchline Campground  
- 3D: Vector Heights, Angle Ranch, Fractal Timberline  
- 2D: Flatiron Fold, Sketchline Fields, Paperbark Woods
